Projects

Twitter-like Social Network Website

A Twitter-like social network website where users can log into their accounts and publish posts. Users can follow/unfollow other users. Users can see other users' posts and like/unlike the posts.

E-commerce Auction Site

An e-commerce auction site where users can log into their accounts, post new auction listings, place bids on listings, comment on listings, and add listings to their “watchlist”. Users can close their listings and the highest bidder wins the auction.

Email App

An email app where users can log into their accounts, send emails to other users, receive emails from others, send replies to received emails, and archive / unarchive emails.

Google Search All-in-One

Combining Google search, Google image search, and Google advanced search UIs into a single web app.

Mini Encyclopedia

A Wikipedia-like encyclopedia where users can read posts, search posts, add new posts and edit existing posts.

Currency Converter

A currency converter that obtains the current rates using APIs and converts a required amount to a given currency.
